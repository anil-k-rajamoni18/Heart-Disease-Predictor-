Heart Diseases, also called Cardiovascular diseases, are the leading cause of death in all areas of the world except Africa. According to WHO, these diseases are a group of disorders found in heart and blood vessels that includes coronary heart disease, cerebrovascular disease, rheumatic heart disease, etc. Together these diseases resulted in around 17.9 million deaths(31% of all global deaths) since 2016, up from 12.3 million deaths (25.8% of all global deaths) since 1990.
